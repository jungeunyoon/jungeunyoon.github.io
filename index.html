WEATHERVAIN


Achievements:

"Best Overall App" and "Most Original App" - 2014 UT Mobile Apps Demo Day

Status: Someone took our name and we were unable to submit to the app store.

Purpose: What happens when computer science and journalism students come together with the goal of creating mobile apps? You get five creative, smart startups producing beautiful, clever apps in one semester.

Role: On the computer science students' end, we had a little less than two months to pick up iOS mobile development without any instruction. My job was to bridge everyone's individual code into one app, to design the user interface, and to develop the outfit matching algorithm, based on the user's personal taste and current and future weather conditions. There ended up being 5000+ outfit choices through this algorithm.

Value: It taught us to learn and adapt quickly. On the computer science students' end, we had a little less than two months to pick up iOS mobile development without any instruction. And in working with non-programmers, it also taught us to be better communicators and it gave us a sense of the real world work environment.
