WEATHERVAIN


Achievements:

"Best Overall App" and "Most Original App" - 2014 UT Mobile Apps Demo Day

Class: What happens when computer science and journalism students come together with the goal of creating mobile apps? You get five creative, smart startups producing beautiful, clever apps in one semester.

Role: On the computer science students' end, we had a little less than two months to pick up iOS mobile development without any instruction. I bridged everyone's individual code into one app, designed and implemented the user interface, and developed the outfit matching algorithm, based on the user's personal taste and current and future weather conditions. There ended up being 5000+ outfit choices through this algorithm.

Value: It taught me the importance of learning and adapting very quickly in software development. Also, in working with non-programmers, it taught me to be a better communicator and it gave me a sense of the real world work environment.
